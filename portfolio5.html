C4D Animation Works

This page showcases my exploration of Cinema 4D and its related plugins and renderers during my independent research. Due to compatibility issues between different plugins and renderers, the works below utilize V-ray, Corona, and the default renderer of C4D R21.

Microsoft-Style Advertisement

Feeling that the previous animation works were somewhat modest, I created this second animation inspired by Microsoft's advertisements. The most time-consuming part was using abstract deformers like wind to replicate the effects of the original video. The frequent appearance of frosted glass and semi-transparent volumetric fog also made rendering time-consuming.

First C4D Animation Series

Selected three pictures of cats. The main focus here was the effect of viscous liquid simulation, which took a long time to adjust to ensure that the material could accurately display in the correct position after the object deforms.

C4D soft body simulation. The left and right sides show different effects under the same model with different structural lines.

Fracture and elastic objects.

Soft body generated by splines. It took a long time to find the appropriate number of segments and structural lines to achieve a perfectly smooth elastic effect.

A combination of soft body, gas, and liquid simulation. At this point in the project, I discovered that the gas smoke effect only supports the default renderer. After unsuccessful attempts, I had to reset the lighting and materials in the default renderer.

The same liquid simulation effect, but the emitter is made into a more interesting form.

Boxes that deform upon collision.
